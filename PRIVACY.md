# Privacy Policy for wristonic

Last updated: April 11, 2026

`wristonic` is a watchOS app for connecting to a user-provided Subsonic-compatible media server, such as Navidrome.

## Summary

- `wristonic` does not operate a developer-hosted backend for your account or media library.
- `wristonic` does not sell your data.
- `wristonic` does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
- `wristonic` sends data only to the media server you configure, or to Apple services needed to run the app on your device.

## Information the app processes

The app may process the following information in order to work:

- Server address
- Username
- Password
- Library metadata returned by your server, such as artists, albums, tracks, cover art, and internet radio stations
- Playback state, playback history, and playback reporting data
- Downloaded media and related local file metadata
- App settings such as bitrate, offline-only mode, and storage limits

## How information is used

The app uses this information to:

- connect to your selected media server
- authenticate your requests
- browse and display your library
- stream audio from your server
- cache artwork and metadata for performance
- store downloaded media for offline playback
- resume playback and maintain playback state
- send now playing and scrobble-style playback reports to your server when applicable

## Where data is stored

Data is primarily stored on your device.

- Credentials are stored on-device, using the Apple Keychain where available.
- App settings, cached metadata, playback cache, download records, and related state may be stored locally on-device.
- Downloaded audio files and cached artwork are stored locally on-device.

## Network connections

`wristonic` connects to the server address you enter. The app sends only the requests needed to browse your library, stream media, download media, fetch artwork, and report playback activity.

If you enable insecure connections, the app may connect over HTTP instead of HTTPS. In that case, data sent between your device and your server may not be encrypted in transit.

## Data sharing

The developer of `wristonic` does not receive your library, credentials, or playback data unless you choose to send that information separately outside the app.

Your information may be disclosed only to:

- the media server you configure
- Apple, as required to operate the app, device, watchOS, networking, storage, and platform security features

The app does not share data with advertising networks or third-party analytics providers.

## Tracking

`wristonic` does not track you across apps or websites.

## Children’s privacy

`wristonic` is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children through a developer-operated service.

## Your choices

You can:

- remove your server credentials from the app
- delete downloaded media from the app
- clear cached and stored app data from the app
- avoid insecure transport by using HTTPS and leaving insecure connections disabled

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted in this repository.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer through the contact method listed on the app’s App Store page or the project’s GitHub repository.
